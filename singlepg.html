About Us




Every success story begins with a vision. In the year 2013, a young entrepreneur understood the importance of a highly efficient and customized conference
specialist entity and thus was born VenYou with a mandate of Adding Values to each and every event handled by it. Over the last year and a half , VenYou has
grown consistently both horizontally as well as vertically . Delivering customized , cost effective and long term solutions to our clients and in the process
setting new benchmarks of excellence has been the standard operating procedure at VenYou. The fact that we have handled over 100 events of various magnitude,
handled a guest list that has seen successive Prime Ministers and Presidents of India apart from luminaries, political and business dignitaries from all over
the world,is a testimony to our excellence and exceeding expectations of our esteemed clients.
Why VenYou ?
With great work comes great responsibility. The team at VenYou believes in this adage and we put in optimum resources, latest technology, best of experience
and personalized services to efficiently organize our events and to ensure that we add value to the original outcome of the event . We focus on not just organizing
a gathering , rather, we endeavour to create a successful convergence of people to achieve a result that not only meets all the expectations of the client.
The 5 plus years and 100 plus successful events , make our team one of the most prepared group of professionals that Indian MICE industry can claim of .
We strongly benefit from our positive outlook and a Can do attitude. With a wide ranging experience, our team understands the needs and goals of client events.
Our Vision
"To become the most preferred 'Meetings Services'company for our stakeholders (customers, participants, employees, vendors, shareholders and society) and
double our value (turnover and profits) every two years till 2020".
Our mission
Mandate is Adding Value to everything that we do. We constantly achieve excellence by combining the elements of energy, strategy, creativity and technology
to exceed expectations of our clients. Our commitment to our mission of Adding value is absolute and through our immense experience, we optimally
achieve the purpose of conducting the client event and we put strong emphasis on not just hassle free organization of the event, achieving the client long term objectives.
